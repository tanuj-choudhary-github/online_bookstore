# online_bookstore

---

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing an **Online Book Store dataset** using a combination of **Microsoft Excel and SQL**. It covers data storage, querying, and analysis of customer behavior, book inventory, and sales performance.

The project demonstrates how structured data can be used to extract meaningful insights such as **top customers, best-selling authors, and inventory management**.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* To manage and analyze book store data efficiently  
* To perform SQL queries for business insights  
* To track customer purchases and spending behavior  
* To analyze book sales by genre and author  
* To monitor stock levels after order fulfillment  
* To identify high-value customers and top-selling books  

---

## 🔍 Steps Performed

* Created structured datasets in Excel (CSV format)  
* Designed relational tables (Customers, Books, Orders)  
* Imported data into MySQL database  
* Applied SQL queries to solve business problems  
* Performed data analysis using SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY, and aggregate functions  

---

## 📊 SQL Analysis & Insights

### 📚 Book Analysis

* Retrieved all books in the **Fiction genre**  
* Identified books published after **1950**  
* Found the **top 3 most expensive Fantasy books**  

---

### 👤 Customer Analysis

* Listed customers from a specific city (**Austinbury**)  
* Identified customers who spent **more than $30**  
* Found the **highest spending customer**  

---

### 🛒 Order Analysis

* Extracted orders placed in **November 2023**  
* Calculated total quantity of books sold by each author  

---

### 📦 Inventory Analysis

* Calculated remaining stock after fulfilling all orders  
* Helped understand inventory usage and restocking needs  

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Fiction and Fantasy genres have strong demand  
* A small group of customers contributes to higher revenue  
* Certain authors generate more total sales volume  
* Monthly analysis helps track seasonal order patterns  
* Stock levels decrease significantly for popular books  
* High-value customers can be targeted for marketing
